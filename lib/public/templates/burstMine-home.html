Burst mine

A graphical distributed plotter/miner for Burst.

What is Burst?

Burst is a crypto-currency based on a Proof of HDD Capacity (PoC) algorithm rather than on a traditional Proof of Work (PoW) algorithm. Thus, the only resource needed to mine this coin is HDD space.

Getting started

The only steps required to mine Burst are:

  • Find some free space on your HDDs.
  • Prepare this space (plotting).
  • Configure your miner (mining).
  • Start mining!
